Revised Spell Names by @red man & @xJ4cks
April 2025 - For FF4 Ultima Plus
--------------------------------
To standardize the spell lists,
this patch makes the following changes:

    Heal --> Esuna
    Pearl --> Holy
    White --> Holy2
    Holy --> Holy3
    Aero --> Wind1
    HiWind --> Wind2
    Psych --> Aspir

Esuna, Aero, and Psych are changed where they occur in the game dialog.
