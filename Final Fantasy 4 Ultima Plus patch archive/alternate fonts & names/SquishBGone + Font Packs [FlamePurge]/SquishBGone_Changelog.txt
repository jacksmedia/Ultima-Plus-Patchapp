NAME DIFFERENCES
----------------
These were the used multi-letter tiles, or "squish tiles," originating from FF4 Ultima Plus.
il, li, ll, ti, it

The following squish tiles were invented for SquishBGone. (An oxymoron statement, I know, but I tried hard to make them look natural and readable.)
tt ($CA), EG ($CD), lar ($D2D3), lemen ($D4D5D6D7)
